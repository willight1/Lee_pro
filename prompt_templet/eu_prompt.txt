Required Fields:
1. issuing_country: Always "EU"

3. tariff_type: Always "Antidumping"
4. tariff_rate: The ad valorem duty rate (as decimal, e.g., 21.5 for 21.5%)
5. effective_date_from: The date the regulation enters into force (format: YYYY-MM-DD)
6. effective_date_to: Leave blank unless expiry date is specified

12. note: Include TARIC additional code and Minimum Import Prices (MIPs) information

- MIP (Minimum Import Price): Extract from Article 1, paragraph 3 table showing product ranges and EUR/tonne prices
- EFFECTIVE DATE: Usually "the day following publication" - calculate from regulation date
- RIP (Review Investigation Period): Look for "review investigation period" or "RIP" in Section 1.4

Instructions:
- Extract information for EACH company mentioned in Article 1 paragraph 4 AND paragraph 5 tables
- Include both individually named companies AND "All other [country] companies" entries

- For dates, use YYYY-MM-DD format
